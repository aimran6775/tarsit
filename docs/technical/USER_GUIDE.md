## For Customers

### Getting Started

1. **Create an Account**
   - Click "Get started" or "Sign up"
   - Enter your email, password, and name
   - Verify your email address
   - Start exploring!

2. **Search for Businesses**
   - Use the search bar on the homepage
   - Enter what you're looking for (e.g., "hair salon")
   - Add your location (city or ZIP code)
   - Browse results or switch to map view

3. **Filter Results**
   - Use category filters
   - Filter by price range ($, $$, $$$)
   - Filter by minimum rating
   - Sort by relevance, rating, or distance

### Finding Businesses

#### Search Features
- **Text Search:** Search by business name or description
- **Location Search:** Find businesses near you
- **Category Browse:** Explore by category
- **Map View:** See businesses on an interactive map

#### Business Details
Each business page shows:
- Business information and description
- Photos and gallery
- Services and pricing
- Business hours
- Location and map
- Reviews and ratings
- Contact information

### Reviews

#### Writing a Review
1. Navigate to a business page
2. Click "Write a Review"
3. Rate the business (1-5 stars)
4. Add a title and comment
5. Optionally upload photos
6. Submit your review

#### Review Guidelines
- Be honest and constructive
- Focus on your experience
- Avoid personal attacks
- Include helpful details

### Messaging

#### Starting a Conversation
1. Go to a business page
2. Click "Message" or "Chat"
3. Send your message
4. Receive real-time responses

#### Chat Features
- Real-time messaging
- Typing indicators
- Read receipts
- Image/file sharing
- Message history

### Appointments

#### Booking an Appointment
1. Find a business that accepts appointments
2. View available services
3. Select a service and time slot
4. Add any special requests
5. Confirm your booking

#### Managing Appointments
- View upcoming appointments in your dashboard
- Cancel appointments (with notice)
- Receive appointment reminders
- View appointment history

### Favorites

#### Saving Businesses
- Click the heart icon on any business
- Access favorites from your dashboard
- Get notifications about favorite businesses

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't log in?**
- Check your email and password
- Verify your email address
- Try password reset

**Search not working?**
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different search term
- Clear browser cache

**Messages not sending?**
- Check your connection
- Refresh the page
- Try logging out and back in

**Appointment booking failed?**
- Check if the time slot is still available
- Verify business hours
- Contact the business directly
