## Quick Test Guide

### Login as a Business Owner
1. Go to `/business/login`
2. Enter any email from above (e.g., `worldowner1@tarsit.com`)
3. Enter password: `Tarsit1234!`
4. You'll be redirected to the Business Dashboard

### Business Dashboard Features
- **Overview** - View stats, recent appointments, reviews
- **Appointments** - Manage customer bookings
- **Reviews** - Respond to customer reviews
- **Photos** - Upload cover image, logo, gallery photos
- **Services** - Manage services, pricing, duration
- **Messages** - Chat with customers
- **Team** - Invite team members with permissions
- **Hours** - Set business operating hours
- **Settings** - Appointment settings, enable/disable bookings
